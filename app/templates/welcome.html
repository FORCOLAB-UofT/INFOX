INFOX

Forks Overview



INFOX is a tool that can generate an overview of active forks in a project on Github and identify not-merged features in forks.

For a quick start, you can reach Find page. And we recommend you to login with your github account to follow the projects & forks, load data from your own account.

Now, the INFOX is just a test version and we will improve continuously. So, your feedback is really helpful for us. Feel free to contact us !
